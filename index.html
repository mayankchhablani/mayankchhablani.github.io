About the Visualization

In this project Hybrid Structure of Narrative Spectrum is employed, which is: Interactive Slide-Show in order to narrate the visual story of Restaurant Business i.e, restaurant reviews via YELP dataset. In this slideshow visual consistency is maintained. Both parameters and triggers are used in all of the visualizations.

Fundamental Elements of Narrative Visualization

  1. Scenes

    2. The slides that make up the presentation. These slides can be navigated through guiding arrows in the bottom-corner. Press Esc to get an overview of all story points.

  2. Annotations

    3. Various tooltips and labels are used in order to restaurant categories and reviews.

  3. Parameters & Triggers

    4. Zoomable Sunburst Visualization where each mouse click on a region is a trigger for the path and arc parameters.

The Yelp Dataset

​​Starting a restaurant is exciting, it’s also time-consuming and one of the toughest businesses to successfully launch. So, understanding your clients is atmost important because, reviews and rating given by client drives the business. This interactive slideshow dives into the publicly available Yelp Academic Dataset which includes a large set of reviews client interactions across multiple industries and regions. By looking into restaurant review reviews and types, we will be able to know which restaurant business may become successfull.
